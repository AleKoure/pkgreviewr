Look for strengths aligned with rOpenSci package expectations: complete package structure, helpful README/examples, roxygen2 documentation, vignettes, and testthat coverage.
Use tidyverse and r-pkgs principles as supporting cues for clarity, naming, consistency, and maintainability.
Keep the body tight and actionable.
